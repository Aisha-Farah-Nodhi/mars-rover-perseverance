What's Mars Rover

Mars Rover is a motor vehicle designed for traveling on the surface of Mars.Over the years,a multitude of space vehicles have been sent to the Red Planet for exploration and each of these aircraft possess their own specialties.Rovers have wheels which enables them to land as well as locomote on Mar's surface.It has immensely helped scientists in their quest to understand what different parts of the Planet are made of.

What will the Perseverance rover do?

  • Collect samples of Martian rock and soil
  • Store samples in sealed tube which is ferried back to Earth for detailed analysis
  • Conduct the search for ancient life in Mars
  • Seek for biosignatures

Launching Vehicle:

A launching vehicle provides the velocity needed by a spacecraft to escape Earth's gravity and set it on it's course for Mars. The Atlas V-541 was used for the launching of the rover Perseverance. This extraordinary spacecraft stands 191 feet(58 meters)tall which is as tall as a 19 story building.It weighs about 1.17 million pounds.
